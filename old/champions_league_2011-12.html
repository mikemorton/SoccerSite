2011-12 UEFA Champions League Results Calculator

Quickly figure out what it will take for your team to advance to the next round.

Enter some scores below and the standings will automatically update according to the Official Tie-Breaking Procedures.
Tied teams are ranked according to the following criteria:
  1. Points from Matches between Tied Teams
  2. Goal Differential from Matches between Tied Teams
  3. Goals Scored in Matches between Tied Teams
  4. Away Goals Scored in Matches between Tied Teams
  5. Goal Difference from all Group Matches
  6. Goals Scored in all Group Matches
  7. UEFA Ranking Co-Efficient (not included in this calculator)
The top two teams in each group advance to the next round. Third place teams advance to the Europa League round of 32.
