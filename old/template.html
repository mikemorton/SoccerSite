2010 World Cup Results Calculator

Quickly figure out what it will take for your team to advance to the next round.

Enter some scores below and the standings will automatically update according to the Official World Cup Tie-Breaking Procedures.
Teams are ranked according to the following criteria:
  1. Points
  2. Goal Differential
  3. Goals Scored
  4. Points in matches between tied teams
  5. Goal Differential in matches between tied teams
  6. Goals Scored in matches between tied teams
  7. Drawing of lots organized by the FIFA Organizing Committee
The top two teams in each group advance to the next round. Scroll to the bottom to play with Knock-Out Stage scenarios.
